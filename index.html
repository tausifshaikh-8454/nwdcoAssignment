About Us

Khar Education Society

Khar Education Society, a Gujarati Minority Welfare Trust, was formed in 1934. It took over 'Bal-Mandir' which had started in 1930, added further classes, and named it 'Sharda Mandir' in 1933 at Khar, Mumbai and brought it under its banner. "My Pupil's Own School" was started by late Shri Jahangirji Vakil and Shrimati Kunvarbai J. Vakil, at the instance of our first Prime Minister Shri. Jawaharlal Nehru in 1929 at Pune (The school shifted to Vile Parle, Mumbai, in 1931). It amalgamated with Sharda Mandir. Then it was named Shri M.M. Pupil's Own School and Sharda Mandir.'

Vision

To provide high-quality, inclusive academic environment for honing innate talents and enhancing potential through value-based education to transform students into global citizens.

Mission

To provide opportunities for holistic learning experiences to every student to become a lifelong learner, innovator, and critical thinker. To develop skills to work towards the overall development of students besides instilling in them the confidence to pursue excellence in all aspects of life.

Values

Core Values: Team KES is driven by these intrinsic

  • Knowledge and character building
  • Excellence
  • Social responsibility

We have impacted the lives of our students in a positive way, helping them carve out their individual niches as successful and responsible adults in society.
